## Global generation requirements

Every generated image should follow these defaults unless a scene-specific prompt overrides them:

```text
Aspect ratio: 16:9
Target size: 1920x1080
Dark fantasy visual novel scene illustration
Painterly semi-realistic concept art
Cinematic composition
High detail
Grounded, serious, mature tone
No anime style
No cartoon style
No comic-book outlines
No UI
No dialogue box
No readable text
No logo
No watermark
```

Composition requirement:

```text
Keep the lower 25–30% of the image visually calm, slightly darker and free of important faces, hands, clues or hero props, suitable for a visual novel dialogue box overlay.
```

Lighting requirement:

```text
Use one dominant light source and one secondary contrasting light source.
Prefer cold blue-grey ambience with restrained warm amber highlights.
```

Scene construction requirement:

```text
Every scene must have one clear hero element.
Capture a specific moment of action or reaction rather than a static pose.
Use natural body language and believable interaction with props.
Let the environment communicate part of the story.
```

Lighthouse requirement:

```text
The lighthouse and its internal routes must read as one coherent, structurally believable engineered landmark.
Use weathered stone, dark iron, restrained brass, optical glass, chains, salt, lenses and maintenance structures.
Avoid surreal geometry, impossible stairs, duplicate lighthouse silhouettes and unrelated fantasy architecture.
```

# Remaining workflow

For each accepted expanded-story image:

1. Save the final accepted file using a stable project filename.
2. Import it into Narrium.
3. Assign it to the correct scene.
4. Verify crop and dialogue-box readability inside the application.
5. Mark `Imported into Narrium` in this roadmap.
6. Perform one chronological visual continuity pass after all imports.

The final continuity pass should verify:

- Lio, Mira, Cael, Voss and Noa identity consistency,
- Lighthouse exterior silhouette consistency,
- Lighthouse interior engineering consistency,
- cold white-blue versus warm amber lighting language,
- absence of duplicate lighthouse structures,
- no important faces or story props hidden behind the dialogue UI,
- correct narrative direction in movement scenes.
