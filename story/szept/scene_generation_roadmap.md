# Scene Generation Roadmap — Whisper Beneath the Lighthouse

## Purpose

This document tracks background / scene CG generation for the expanded English version of **Whisper Beneath the Lighthouse**.

It should be used together with:

- `story/szept/art_architecture_guidelines.md`
- `story/szept/art_bible.md`
- `story/szept/art_generation_notes.md`
- `story/szept/art_prop_guidelines.md`
- `story/szept/art_scene_guidelines.md`

The goal is to generate one consistent visual novel scene illustration for every scene that does not yet have an accepted background.

---

## Global generation requirements

Every generated image should follow these defaults unless a scene-specific prompt overrides them:

```text
Aspect ratio: 16:9
Target size: 1920x1080
Dark fantasy visual novel scene illustration
Painterly semi-realistic concept art
Cinematic composition
High detail
Grounded, serious, mature tone
No anime style
No cartoon style
No comic-book outlines
No UI
No dialogue box
No readable text
No logo
No watermark
```

Composition requirement:

```text
Keep the lower 25–30% of the image visually calm, slightly darker and free of important faces, hands, clues or hero props, suitable for a visual novel dialogue box overlay.
```

Lighting requirement:

```text
Use one dominant light source and one secondary contrasting light source.
Prefer cold blue-grey ambience with restrained warm amber highlights.
```

Scene construction requirement:

```text
Every scene must have one clear hero element.
Capture a specific moment of action or reaction rather than a static pose.
Use natural body language and believable interaction with props.
Let the environment communicate part of the story.
```

Prop requirement:

```text
Hero props must look like real, used objects belonging to Veyr before they look like fantasy artifacts.
Use salt deposits, moisture damage, worn edges, age marks and handcrafted imperfections.
Do not oversize props or add magical glow unless explicitly required.
Keep passive props understated.
```

Lighthouse requirement:

```text
Whenever the lighthouse is visible, it must read as one coherent monumental structure.
The base, tower shaft and lantern room must be visually connected.
Do not create a second separate lighthouse silhouette.
The lighthouse should read as a lighthouse first, then as a sacred or fortress-like landmark.
```

Supernatural requirement:

```text
The supernatural should emerge from believable machinery, architecture and materials such as glass, brass, stone, iron, chains, salt, lenses and light.
Avoid generic spell effects, glowing runes, fantasy aura explosions or unrelated magical energy.
```

# Per-scene workflow

For each scene:

1. Review the scene dialogue and choices in the current Narrium JSON export.
2. Re-read the relevant art guidelines.
3. Define:
   - hero element,
   - active and passive props,
   - exact action moment,
   - dominant and secondary light sources,
   - character placement,
   - environmental story anchors,
   - scene-specific negative constraints.
4. Prepare one complete English generation prompt.
5. Generate the first image.
6. Review:
   - character continuity,
   - composition and VN readability,
   - hero-element clarity,
   - prop hierarchy,
   - lighthouse and architecture continuity,
   - unwanted text, UI, extra objects or duplicate landmarks.
7. Prepare an edit-only prompt if corrections are needed.
8. Accept the final image.
9. Import it into Narrium and assign it to the correct scene.
10. Update the checkboxes in this roadmap.
